# Modulo-3 Prompt :
A weathered mountaineer in his 40s stands still on a rocky alpine ridge, wearing a yellow puffer jacket, climbing harness and technical backpack. He gazes off-camera toward the horizon, contemplative and calm. Behind him, jagged snow-capped peaks rise into shifting fog and low clouds.

Subtle, slow motion only: wisps of fog drift and roll across the distant peaks, a few strands of his hair move gently in the cold wind, his chest rises softly with slow breathing, and his eyes shift almost imperceptibly. The clouds swirl slowly around the summits. His body remains largely still — patient, grounded.

Camera: extremely slow, almost static push-in (subtle dolly forward) with faint handheld micro-movement. Cinematic, editorial documentary aesthetic.

Lighting: soft diffused overcast daylight, cool tones, natural muted color grade, filmic grain, shallow depth of field with the peaks softly out of focus.

Mood: quiet, meditative, epic scale, solitude, endurance. No fast movement, no camera shake, no dramatic action.

Duration: 5-10 seconds, seamless slow motion.
